How DevClub operates

Four pillars, one program.

01

Build for Campus

Students design and ship tools that solve real problems for ugSoT itself — attendance, portals, dashboards.

→ A living portfolio the whole campus uses
02

Learn by Collaborating

Every project runs in small teams with code reviews, shared repos, and rotating ownership.

→ Real teamwork habits, before the first job
03

Learn from Industry

Recurring sessions, reviews, and mentorship from practicing engineers outside ugSoT.

→ A realistic, current picture of the field
04

Build What Interests You

Beyond campus tools, members pursue self-directed projects aligned with their own interests.

→ Genuine ownership and sustained motivation
